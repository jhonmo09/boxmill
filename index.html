About

Boxmill has been successfully developing and investing in London’s real estate market since 2004, completing exclusive schemes with a value of over £100 million.

We have consistently created inspirational and innovatively designed real estate, delivering excellent returns to satisfied investors and delighting end users.

Through consultancy services, we offer our professional expertise and knowledge to funds, developers and investors.

Opportunities to work with like-minded partners are welcomed and we have considerable experience in assembling joint venture structures and funding agreements.

In recent years, we have diversified our focus and acquired specialist knowledge of Colombia, now enjoying an optimistic post-conflict outlook and offering exciting scope for investment.

Boxmill operates from our offices in London and Bogotá.

Emerald Fund:

Emerald Fund is our fixed term closed investment vehicle and offers an innovative opportunity for low risk private equity and debt investment in one of the world’s most exciting counties, capitalising on Colombia’s real estate asset class and the excellent medium-term growth prospects presented by the market and national economy.

Condor Fund:

Condor Fund is our development vehicle and offers an innovative opportunity for low risk private equity and debt investment in one of the world’s most exciting counties, capitalising on Colombia’s real estate asset class and the excellent medium-term growth prospects presented by the market and national economy.

Condor seeks to capitalise on the significant opportunity to raise development standards in Colombia through introducing European influences, design and product quality.
